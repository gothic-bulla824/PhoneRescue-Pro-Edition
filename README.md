## 💡 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you run into trouble, try these solutions before seeking further help.

*   Connection failures: Use the original Apple cable that came with your phone. Some third-party cables only charge the phone and do not transfer data.
*   Software crashes: Restart your computer and ensure no other backup software runs in the background.
*   Device not found: Check that you installed the latest version of iTunes. Windows needs the drivers inside iTunes to communicate with your hardware.
*   Scanning stops: If the scan freezes, disconnect your phone, close the application, reopen it, and start the scan again. Keep your phone awake during the scan to prevent interruptions.

## 📝 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this software recover permanently deleted photos?**
Yes. PhoneRescue Pro scans the unallocated space on your device memory where deleted tags exist. It can often reconstruct file fragments if the original data has not been overwritten by new information.

**Will this process wipe my current data?**
No. The recovery process is read-only. It reads the existing data on your phone and does not delete or change your current files.

**How long does a scan take?**
The time depends on the storage size of your phone. A phone with 256 GB of storage takes longer to scan than a phone with 64 GB. Plan for 5 to 20 minutes for a complete scan.

**Why does the software ask for my passcode?**
The software needs access to your backup files to decrypt the data. Providing your passcode allows the software to read encrypted messages and private contacts.

**Can I stop the recovery if I change my mind?**
You can click the Cancel or Stop button at any time during the scan or recovery process. This will pause the task safely.
